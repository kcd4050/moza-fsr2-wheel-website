MOZA FSR Formula Wheel

Experience precision and performance

280mm Formula Wheel, Built for Ultimate Racing

The MOZA FSR2 wheel is a formula-style racing wheel designed for maximum immersion and an ultimate racing experience.

4.3-inch Touchscreen LCD display

The upgraded 4.3-inch touchscreen supports intuitive swiping actions (up, down, left, right), enabling quick changes to dashboard styles and seamless switching between different UI interfaces during a race.

  • 4.3-inch Display
  • 480p Resolution
  • 16.7M Colors
  • 60Hz Refresh Rate

6 RGB Signal Lights

The MOZA FSR2 Formula Wheel is equipped with 6 RGB signal lights, offering up to 16.7 million color combinations. These lights provide real-time track alerts, such as yellow, blue, and red flags, offering intuitive cues like accident warnings or overtaking signals to keep drivers aware of track conditions. The lighting modes are customizable to suit different race regulations and track requirements.

Innovative Craftsmanship, Premium Quality

Featuring a sturdy frame made of 5mm high-strength carbon fiber and 3mm magnetic shifter paddles, enjoy a weighty feel and trusted reliability.

The perforated microfiber leather ensure both a premium feel and exceptional control.

The rear casing is made from CNC machined aluminum alloy, providing a refined metallic texture with high strength and rigidity.

6-paddle Design

The MOZA FSR2 Formula Wheel features a 6-paddle design. In addition to the shift and dual-clutch paddles, it includes an extra pair of paddles for quick activation of DRS and KERS systems, along with flexible adjustment of driving modes and vehicle settings to meet a variety of operational needs.

Customized Quick Release

MOZA's quick release system ensures a seamless wheel transition in seconds. Enjoy a stable, solid connection that works across the whole MOZA Racing ecosystem.
